Bio

I am an M.S. in Computer Science student at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities with a research focus on field robotics and multi-agent systems. I conduct research in the Next Generation Robotics Laboratory and am currently a planetary sampling automation intern at NASA Ames Research Center. I've worked as a teaching assistant for courses in Python, Java, algorithms and data structures, and artificial intelligence.
